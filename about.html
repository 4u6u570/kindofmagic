About Us

Founded in 2010, PASTO is an agency consultancy producing a diverse range of work spanning across brand identity, art direction, spatial and web design. Located in Rosario (Santa Fe), Argentina, we work within a range of sectors from the fashion and beauty, property, F&B and product fields.

This simplified, yet considered, aesthetic has allowed us to produce engaging work for an array of clients from across the world ranging from startups to global brands.

Each project begin with a unique approach, a meticulous focus on crafting a strong narrative and conceptual grounding. Through our attention to detail, commercial knowledge and strategic reasoning, we are able to fulfil and look to exceed client expectation whilst producing throught-provoking yet considered design.

Our process support our clients by managing briefs in their entirety from concept and design, through to production and final delivery.
